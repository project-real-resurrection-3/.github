## The Universal Baseline: All 164 WTO/TRIPS Members

Even if a country isn't listed above, the **TRIPS Agreement** creates a universal minimum baseline:

**TRIPS Article 13** (mirroring Berne Convention Three-Step Test) requires that all exceptions:
1. Apply to special cases only
2. Not conflict with normal exploitation of the work
3. Not unreasonably prejudice the legitimate interests of the rights holder

The RR3 Community Project satisfies all three in every jurisdiction because:

| Condition | Our Situation |
|-----------|--------------|
| Special case only | âœ… Specific abandoned game, specific shutdown |
| Normal exploitation | âœ… EA is **not exploiting** â€” servers are shut down |
| Prejudice to rights holder | âœ… EA **abandoned** this product |

**Additionally, TRIPS Article 10** establishes that computer programs are protected as literary works â€” meaning all the literary work exceptions (research, interoperability, private study) apply to software in all member states.
